Winja Blogs | September 2020

Sep 5, 2020 winja blogs

We conducted a technical blog writing workshop for women participants.

Our first #WinjaBlogs session involved 6 women participants and three mentors. Our mentors included Christa Anderson, Akash Mahajan and Saikat Datta. Each mentee came prepared with their draft copy of a technical blog. The mentors had a one-on-one interaction with their mentees for a duration of 20 - 30 minutes. During the discussion, the mentees tried to identify ways to enrich their technical blog content. The session ended with a group discussion and participant feedback.
