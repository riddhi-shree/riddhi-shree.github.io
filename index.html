About Us

We are a group of women and men, working together, with an aim to embrace diversity. We aim to create a thriving space that allows people to come together, think together and create awareness around latest technological trends. We aim to build capabilities in the field of Information Security.

Some of the events that we have conducted so far include capture-the-flag (CTF) competitions, blog-writing workshop, and online technical talks.
